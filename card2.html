Anthonys Nose


Anthony's Nose is a peak along the Hudson River at the north end of Westchester County, New York.

Since I love going hiking and photo-shooting one of my friends recommended visiting Anthony's Nose place. My friends and I went hiking last year. It was almost sunset time, and the view was beautiful. We had fun photo-shooting. This is one of my favorite pictures of that place.
