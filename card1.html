Kaaterskill Falls


Kaaterskill Falls is a two-stage waterfall on Spruce Creek in the eastern Catskill Mountains of New York.

Tips for Hiking at Kaaterskill Falls

  • Only follow the marked trail signs.
  • Do not climb on the rocks under or around the waterfall.
  • Wear sturdy shoes for hikes.
  • Bring water and snacks.
  • Do not light a campfire.
